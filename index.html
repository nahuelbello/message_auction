Sounds good but, how can I earn money with this?

When you place a bid in Message Auction, you not only showcase your message but also acquire shares in the system. Every time someone outbids you, a portion of the bid is distributed among all previous participants proportionally to the shares they hold. As the auction grows, your shares generate passive ETH income, and early participation rewards you with special bonuses that further boost your earnings.

This is how you can participate:

First, ensure you have installed your wallet's extension in your browser (Chrome, Brave, etc.) and created a new wallet if you don't already have one. You can use any wallet, but we recommend MetaMask or Trust.

Once your wallet is set up, click on "Connect Wallet" and confirm.

Once you've connected your wallet, start thinking about your message. You can write anything without limitations or censorship (except for a 50-character limit). Then, enter the ETH amount you want to bid and click "Place bid" to confirm the transaction. (Keep in mind you must have enough funds in your account to cover the gas fees and the 4% founder fee‚Äîfor example, if you bid 1 ETH, you should have over 1.05 ETH.)

Once your bid is placed, your message will remain the main banner until someone outbids you. As more users participate, your ETH rewards will accumulate.

As your earnings increase, you can withdraw them at any time. Simply reconnect your wallet (it must be the same address used for bidding), click on "Withdraw," confirm the transaction, and your ETH will be transferred in a few minutes.
