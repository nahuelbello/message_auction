When you place a bid in Message Auction, you're not only showcasing your message but also acquiring shares in the system. Every time someone outbids you, a portion of the bid is distributed among all previous participants, proportional to the shares they hold. As the auction grows, your shares generate passive ETH income. Additionally, early participation rewards you with special bonuses that further boost your earning potential.
