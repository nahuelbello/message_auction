How This Works

In this DApp, you submit a bid that must exceed the current bid by at least 0.01 ETH. Each address can only participate once. When your bid is successful, your message appears in the banner for everyone to see.

The new payment is equally distributed among all previous participants. This means that if you join early, you can earn passive income whenever someone places a higher bid.

The smart contract is fully transparent and deployed on Ethereum, ensuring that every transaction is public and verifiable. You can withdraw your accumulated funds at any time.

This incentive structure makes the platform highly attractive for investors and users alike. Place your bid, claim the banner, and enjoy a truly decentralized and rewarding experience!
