Everything You Need to Know

Your most common questions, answered!

1. What is Message Auction? ▶

Message Auction is a decentralized application (dApp) where users bid with short messages in ETH. The highest bid’s message is displayed on the main banner, and all participants earn rewards based on their shares.

2. How do I place a bid? ▶

1. Connect your wallet by clicking “Connect Wallet.”
2. Enter your message and ETH amount (≥0.01 ETH above the current bid).
3. Click “Place Bid” and confirm in your wallet.
4. If successful, your message appears on the banner immediately.

3. Why do I need to connect a wallet? ▶

Because this is a blockchain-based dApp, you need a wallet to send transactions (bids, withdrawals) and to receive any ETH rewards. It also proves ownership of your funds and secures them.

4. What is the minimum bid increment? ▶

You must outbid the current highest bid by at least 0.01 ETH. The very first bid must be at least 0.01 ETH if there’s no current bid yet.

5. What happens if someone outbids me? ▶

Your message will no longer be displayed on the banner. However, you keep the shares you earned from your bid, which means you still receive a portion of future bids as rewards. You can always place a higher bid to reclaim the banner.

6. How are rewards distributed? ▶

Each time a new bid comes in, 4% goes to the founder, and the rest is distributed proportionally among all share holders. Your portion depends on how many shares you hold compared to the total shares.

7. What are “shares” and how do I get them? ▶

Shares represent your stake in the auction. You receive shares each time you place a bid, based on your bid amount and any applicable Early Bird Bonus. The more shares you have, the larger your portion of each new bid.

8. What is the Early Bird Bonus? ▶

The first 10 unique bidders get a multiplier on their shares. The first 5 receive 1.5x, and the next 5 receive 1.25x. After that, all bids receive a standard 1.0x multiplier.

9. How do I know if I’m still eligible for the Early Bird Bonus? ▶

The contract tracks how many people have already claimed the bonus. If it’s under 10 and you haven’t bid before, you’ll automatically receive the bonus. Once 10 people have claimed it, the bonus is no longer available.

10. Why do I see a “Founder fee transfer failed” error? ▶

This indicates the contract couldn’t transfer the 4% founder fee. It’s rare and usually points to a network or address issue. Try again or wait a bit for the network to clear.

11. What does the founder do with the 4% fee? ▶

The founder fee supports and improves the project, covering development costs and funding potential new features.

12. How do I withdraw my rewards? ▶

1. Connect your wallet.
2. Click “Withdraw.”
3. Confirm the transaction in your wallet.
If you have pending rewards, you’ll receive them in ETH.

13. Why do I see “No shares” or “No funds to withdraw”? ▶

“No shares” appears if you haven’t placed any bid yet (you have zero shares). “No funds to withdraw” means you do have shares but no pending rewards at the moment.

14. Do I have to pay gas fees? ▶

Yes. Every transaction on the blockchain (placing bids, withdrawing rewards) requires gas fees, which depend on the network’s congestion.

15. Why is my transaction stuck or pending? ▶

It could be due to network congestion or low gas fees. Check your wallet or a block explorer to see if you need to speed up the transaction.

16. Where can I see the code? ▶

The whole code is on GitHub, and there’s a direct link to the deployed contract in the “Transparency” section. You can also check the smart contract on Etherscan.

17. Is this code audited? ▶

The project is open-source but may not have a formal third-party audit. You can review the contract yourself or consult a security expert.

18. Can I see all the bids that have been placed? ▶

The dApp shows the latest bids (usually the last 10). For a full history, check the contract’s events on a block explorer or the repository logs.

19. Are the messages moderated? ▶

There's no built-in moderation, it means you can write whatever you want on the banner.

20. Why does my banner message disappear? ▶

If someone outbids you, their message replaces yours. You still keep your shares and can earn from future bids.

21. Can I change my message without placing a new bid? ▶

No. The only way to change the banner message is to place a new, higher bid, ensuring fairness for everyone.

22. How often do I need to refresh to see updates? ▶

The dApp listens for “NewBid” events and updates automatically. If you notice any delay, a manual refresh (F5) will also fetch the latest data.

23. What is the Ownership Simulator? ▶

The Ownership Simulator lets you estimate how many shares you’d gain and what your ownership percentage might be if you place a certain bid, factoring in any Early Bird Bonus.

24. How do I test this on a local or test network? ▶

You can run a local blockchain (e.g., Hardhat, Ganache) or deploy the contract to a testnet (like Goerli) with test ETH. Make sure your wallet is connected to the correct network.

25. Do I lose my shares if I withdraw? ▶

No. Withdrawing only claims your accumulated rewards. Your shares remain the same until you place another bid (which increases them).

26. How can I contact the founder or get support? ▶

Check the “Transparency” section or the links provided (GitHub, LinkedIn). You can open an issue on GitHub or reach out directly via social media.

27. Are there any risks? ▶

All investments involve some degree of risk, and cryptocurrencies can be especially volatile. The price of ETH may fluctuate significantly, and there’s always the possibility of transaction failures or unexpected network issues. It’s recommended that you do your own research and only invest funds you’re comfortable risking.

28. Is there a maximum number of bids? ▶

No. Bids can continue indefinitely. There’s no limit to how many times users can outbid each other.

29. Can I remove my message or undo a bid? ▶

Once a transaction is confirmed on the blockchain, it cannot be undone. If you no longer want your message displayed, someone else must outbid you.

30. How do I see my total shares and pending rewards? ▶

When your wallet is connected, the dApp displays your current shares and pending reward in the main widget. If you have shares, you earn a portion of every new bid.

31. Why can't I connect my wallet? ▶

If your browser lacks an injected provider (e.g., using Chrome/Safari on mobile or a PC without a wallet extension like MetaMask), you won’t be able to connect.
